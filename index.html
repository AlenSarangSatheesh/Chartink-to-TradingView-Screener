05 / FAQ

Questions, answered

Which websites does it work on? +
Exactly three: chartink.com (where redirects start), tradingview.com and screener.in (where they land, and where Ctrl+Q switching works). The extension runs on no other sites.
Do I need a TradingView or Screener account? +
No. The extension simply opens public stock pages on those platforms. If you're logged in, you'll see your own layouts and watchlists as usual.
Why does it need permissions for those three sites? +
To read the stock symbol from the page you're on and open the same stock on your chosen platform. That's the entire job. There's no data collection — see the privacy policy.
Can I open a stock on multiple platforms at once? +
Yes — tick any combination of TradingView, Screener.in and Chartink in the popup. One click on a Chartink stock opens it on all of them, each in its own reusable tab.
Is it free? +
Completely free, no sign-up, no premium tier. Found a bug or have an idea? Email the developer — feedback drives the roadmap.
